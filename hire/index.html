What domain expertise actually looks like

Not abstract "AI workers." People with real careers, catching the things a generalist would miss.

Clinician · Healthcare AI

Reviewing a diagnostic model's output for a rare pulmonary condition

A physician with 10 years in respiratory medicine catching a false negative that a data labeler with no clinical background couldn't have flagged.

Attorney · Legal AI

Annotating contract clauses in a document-understanding model

A practicing lawyer who knows that "material adverse change" means something specific — and that the model's interpretation would never hold up in court.

CFA · Financial AI

Validating an earnings forecast model against regulatory standards

A chartered analyst who spots that the model is extrapolating from pre-restatement figures — an error a non-specialist reviewer wouldn't catch.

Multilingual reviewer · Regional AI

Catching a culturally offensive framing in a localized model output

A native speaker who identifies that the model's phrasing is grammatically correct but socially unacceptable in that market — something no translation tool surfaces.
