Meet the speakers

Chaya Springer

Director of the Project Management Office, Mitsubishi Power

Chaya leads initiatives focused on operational excellence, project delivery, and continuous improvement across Service Operations. Starting as an intern 13 years ago, she now brings extensive experience in project management, process optimization, and organizational leadership. Outside of work, Chaya is an avid supporter of rescue animals — especially pitbulls — and a dedicated Formula 1 fan, drawn to the sport's precision, strategy, and high-performance culture.

Matt Ellithorpe

Vice President of Service Engineering, Mitsubishi Power Americas

Matthew leads more than 150 engineers and technical specialists supporting a fleet of over 140 advanced-class gas turbines and 400 steam turbines. He owns the strategy and execution of Service Engineering, Monitoring & Diagnostics, and Controls Engineering, while driving initiatives in reliability, digital transformation, cybersecurity, and AI. With more than 20 years in power generation, his expertise spans gas and steam turbine technology, flexible operation, hydrogen and fuel-flexibility upgrades, remote monitoring, and major equipment modernization. He holds B.S. and M.S. degrees in Mechanical Engineering from Union College.

Michael Glover

Senior Vice President of New Generation Systems, Mitsubishi Power Americas

Michael leads the New Generation Systems (NGS) business unit, with scope spanning all Engineering, Project Management, Project Execution, Product Line Management, and Hydrogen Production — over 700 team members focused on GTCC applications and opportunities. Previously he led the Power Generation Service (PGS) Engineering department, responsible for total plant support including new product development, root-cause and corrective action, monitoring and diagnostics, service and outage support, upgrade analysis, design and applications, and master data management.

About the company

Mitsubishi Power Americas, Inc.

Mitsubishi Power Americas, Inc., headquartered in Lake Mary, FL, is creating a future that works for people and the planet through innovative power generation technology and solutions. Our more than 3,500 employees focus on empowering customers in power generation and delivering innovative solutions across North, Central, and South America.

Mitsubishi Power's solutions include gas, steam, and aero-derivative turbines; power trains and power islands; geothermal systems; environmental controls; and services. We also offer solutions that leverage AI-enabled autonomous operation of power plants.

Mitsubishi Power is a power solutions brand of Mitsubishi Heavy Industries, Ltd. (MHI). Headquartered in Tokyo, Japan, MHI is one of the world's leading heavy-machinery manufacturers, with engineering and manufacturing businesses spanning energy, infrastructure, transport, aerospace, and defense.
