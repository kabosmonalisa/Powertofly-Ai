What you can use them for

01

RLHF & human feedback

The feedback your model learns from is only as good as the people providing it. Domain experts bring the industry knowledge that turns human feedback into something your model can actually use.

02

Evaluation & QA

Structured testing against your criteria, across languages and use cases. Domain-matched experts test outputs against real-world expectations, not just benchmark scores.

03

Red teaming

Adversarial testing by people who know where your model is most likely to fail. Our red teamers come from the industries your model serves, so they find the failures that actually matter before your users do.
