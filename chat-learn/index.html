Meet the speakers

Priya N.

Machine Learning Engineer

Priya builds and ships production ML systems, with a focus on evaluation and reliability. She's spent the last several years turning promising prototypes into services teams can actually depend on — and has strong, hard-won opinions about what's worth the effort.

Daniel O.

AI Product Lead

Daniel leads AI product work at the seam of research and real users. He cares less about the newest model and more about the workflow around it — evaluation, feedback loops, and the team habits that keep quality high as things scale.

Sofia M.

Applied Research Scientist

Sofia works on applied evaluation and red teaming, probing where models break before users do. She translates messy research findings into practices engineering teams can put to work the same week.
