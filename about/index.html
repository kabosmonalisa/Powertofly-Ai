About PowerToFly AI

Connecting the world's
talent to the work that
matters most.

For over a decade, we've built one of the world's largest professional communities of over 1.1M people spanning every industry, domain, and background. Today, that community is powering a new mission: making sure AI is built by the people it's meant to serve.

Why we started

PowerToFly was founded in 2014 by Milena Berry and Katharine Zaleski, after Katharine had her first child and found far fewer opportunities to keep building her career than she had before.

That's where PowerToFly's mission was born. Teams that reflect the world around them get better results — so we set out to close that gap, connecting global professionals with companies ready to put their experience to work.

Now we're applying that same idea to AI.

Why this matters now

The best AI will be built by people who reflect the world it serves.

AI learns from human input: the data it's trained on, the feedback that shapes it, the people who define what "good" looks like. The more those people reflect the world the AI is built for, the better it works for everyone.

That's the opportunity we've spent 12 years preparing for. Our community already represents the breadth of perspective, expertise, and experience AI needs to get this right.

The shift

AI is where some of the biggest decisions about access and representation are being made today.

From the start, we set out to close the gap between talent and opportunity. Now we're bringing that work to where it matters most: helping companies build AI with the people who reflect the world it serves.

The community, events, and resources you know from PowerToFly are still here. We're just putting them to work on the problem that needs them most.
