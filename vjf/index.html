How the job fair works

It runs like an in-person job fair — just online. Here's what to expect on the day.

01

Register and upload your resume

Create your free account and add your resume — it uploads straight to your PowerToFly profile so recruiters can find you.

02

Explore the companies hiring

See who's attending and the roles they're filling before the doors open, so you can plan the sessions that matter most to you.

03

Join the live presentations

Each company presents its team, culture, and open roles, then takes your questions live. Cameras are optional, and every session is recorded.

04

Connect and get noticed

Tap "I'm interested" on any company's profile to raise your hand — recruiters follow up with the people who stood out.
